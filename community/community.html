Welcome to the TEPL Community Forum

Welcome to the TEPL Community Forum! This is a place for discussions, questions, and sharing ideas related to TEPL, the Textual Educational Programming Language.

Here are some things you can do on the forum:

  • Ask questions about TEPL syntax and usage
  • Share your TEPL projects and code snippets
  • Discuss best practices for teaching programming with TEPL
  • Connect with other TEPL enthusiasts and educators
